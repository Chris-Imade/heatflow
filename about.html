About Heatflow Experts

We are a locally trusted team of heating and energy specialists based in Wellingborough, Northamptonshire.

Our mission is to make homes warmer, smarter, and more energy efficient — while saving our customers money through modern technology and quality workmanship.

All our engineers are Gas Safe Registered and trained in renewable systems including air source and ground source heat pumps.

Whether it's a full boiler replacement, energy-saving upgrade, or air conditioning installation, we promise reliability, honesty, and exceptional results every time.
