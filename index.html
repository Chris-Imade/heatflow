We are a locally trusted team of heating and energy specialists based in Wellingborough, Northamptonshire.

Our mission is to make homes warmer, smarter, and more energy efficient — while saving our customers money through modern technology and quality workmanship.

All our engineers are Gas Safe Registered and trained in renewable systems including air source and ground source heat pumps.

Our Services

Explore Our Expert Services

Boiler Installation & Servicing

Stay warm all year round with expert boiler installations and maintenance from our certified engineers.

We install, repair, and service all major boiler brands, offering fast turnaround and next-day availability.

  • Combi, system, and regular boilers
  • Full Gas Safe & Building Regulations compliance
  • Free filter and CO detector with every install
  • Next-day installation available
  • System balancing and flushing included

All backed by our 2-Year Workmanship Guarantee and 5-Year Pipework Warranty.

Heat Pumps (Air & Ground Source)

Go green and cut your energy bills with the latest air source and ground source heat pumps.

Our specialists handle everything from design to installation, ensuring efficient, quiet, and reliable systems for your property.

  • Fully accredited installation
  • Ideal for retrofit and new builds
  • Smart thermostat integration
  • Maintenance and performance testing

Join the renewable revolution with Heatflow Experts — your local low-carbon heating specialists.

Air Conditioning & Ventilation

Keep your home cool, fresh, and comfortable all year round.

We supply, install, and maintain energy-efficient air conditioning and ventilation systems for homes and small businesses.

  • Wall-mounted, ducted, and split systems
  • Improved indoor air quality and airflow
  • Annual servicing and filter cleaning
  • F-Gas certified engineers

Smart Thermostats & Zoning

Control your heating from anywhere with smart thermostat technology.

We supply and install Hive, Nest, and Tado systems — helping you save energy and reduce costs.

  • Wireless heating control
  • Room-by-room zoning
  • Setup, app configuration & user training
  • Compatible with major brands and boilers

Energy-Saving Upgrades

Simple upgrades can make a big difference.

We provide energy-saving improvements that enhance system performance and efficiency.

  • TRVs and thermostatic controls
  • Pipe insulation and system balancing
  • Power flushing & filter fitting
  • Water-saving fittings

Plumbing & Maintenance

  • Pipework repairs and upgrades
